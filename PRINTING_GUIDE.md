# Phomu Printing Guide

You can print your own Phomu cards to play physically.

## Option A: Professional Printing (e.g., meinspiel.de)
1. Use the "Export" tool in the Phomu Web-App.
2. Select your song pack and choose "Export as PNG".
3. Upload the images to your preferred printing service. 
   - Recommended size: 59x91mm (Standard Card).

## Option B: DIY Home Printing
1. Export as a PDF sheet.
2. Print on cardstock (at least 300g/m²).
3. Cut along the lines and use card sleeves for a premium feel.

## QR Codes
Each code points to `your-phomu-url.com/play?id=SONG_ID`.
